J.J. Wolf siblings: Meet sister Danielle Wolf

Danielle is the older sister J J Wolf. Interestingly, Danielle is also a tennis player. Danielle won the National Open Doubles championship in Dayton, Ohio, and St. Louis, Mo. in 2014, and was the runner-up of the Regional Segment in St. Louis, Mo. in both singles and doubles in Nov. 2013.

J.J. Wolf siblings: Meet sister Danielle Wolf

Danielle also placed second in Regional Segment in singles in Wisconsin in July 2013, and placed third in the Ohio state tournament in singles as a junior, placing fourth in the state tourney in both their sophomore and freshman seasons.

Achieving more in her career, she is a three-time MVP of Miami Valley Conference and Cincinnati Country Day school and was named first-team all-city and all-state for three years, however, Danielle did not play senior year due to injury

Danielle attended Cincinnati Country Day School where she graduated with honors being voted Best Athlete by her peers.

Danielle shares their parents with her brother J J. Their father is  Jeff Wolf and their mother is Brooke Wolf. Both Jeff and Brooke were Division I athletes as Jeff played basketball and professional tennis after college and Brooke played tennis.

Her younger brother, Jeffrey John (J. J.) Wolf is also an American professional tennis player. He achieved a career-high ATP singles ranking of world No. 83 on 15 August 2022. Wolf played collegiately at The Ohio State University. J J began playing semi-pro tennis in 2016 and three years later, in July 2019, signed with Topnotch Management to play professionally.
