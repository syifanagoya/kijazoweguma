Claudette Ortiz: A Brief Biography

Claudette Ortiz, born on July 21, 1981, in Willingboro, New Jersey, USA, is a well-known singer and model. She is recognized as the female vocalist in the R&B group City High.

Have you ever thought about Claudette Ortiz’s wealth? By late 2016, it was estimated that Claudette’s total value amounted to $300,000, accumulated through her successful music career. Her solo ventures and the group City High equally contributed to her net worth.

Claudette Ortiz’s Net Worth: $300,000

Claudette, who has Puerto Rican heritage, met her future City High bandmates, Ryan Toby and Robbie Pardlo, during high school. The trio was active from 2001 to 2003 and released their debut album, “City High”, in 2001. The album achieved gold status, further boosting Claudette’s net worth.

After the group’s disbandment, she pursued a solo career, releasing her first solo single, “Can’t Get Enough”, in 2006, featuring musician Mase. Claudette also worked on her first album, collaborating with notable artists like Pharrell Williams and Will.i.am.

Following her music career, Claudette ventured into television, appearing in shows like “R&B Divas: Los Angeles” and “If Loving You Is Wrong”, as well as the play “Madea on the Run”. Her television appearances significantly augmented her total value.

In her personal life, Claudette was in a relationship with her City High colleague, Robby Pardlo, during high school. After their split, she began dating another City High member, Ryan Toby, whom she married in 2004. However, the marriage ended in divorce in 2007, after they had two sons together. Additionally, Claudette has a daughter from a previous relationship.
