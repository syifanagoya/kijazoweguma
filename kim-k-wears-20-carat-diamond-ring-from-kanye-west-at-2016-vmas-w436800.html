Kim K. Wears 20-Carat Diamond Ring From Kanye West at 2016 VMAs

Taking a cue from Rihanna! Kim Kardashian shined bright like a diamond — or, at least, her left hand did at the 2016 MTV Video Music Awards in NYC on Sunday, August 28. And that’s because she sported her brand-new 20-carat diamond ring from her husband, Kanye West.

PHOTOS: See What Everyone Wore to the VMAs Afterparties

In lieu of her engagement ring — a not-so-shabby 15-carat stunner by Lorraine Schwartz — the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 35, rocked the gargantuan rock on her left ring finger. While, understandably, it was hard to focus on anything else, she also modeled a skintight LBD, lace-up heels and a pair of gold necklaces.

PHOTOS: MTV VMAs 2016 Red Carpet Style: What the Stars Wore

But back to the bling: A source tells Us Weekly that the ring, also a Lorraine Schwartz creation, boasts an emerald-cut center stone that’s categorized as D flawless, Type IIa — the most valued and purest type of diamond. (Fun fact: Less than 2 percent of the world’s diamonds are in that range.) The whopper is set in an incredibly thin band studded with teeny pave diamonds. And the price tag? According to the source, an estimated $10 million.
