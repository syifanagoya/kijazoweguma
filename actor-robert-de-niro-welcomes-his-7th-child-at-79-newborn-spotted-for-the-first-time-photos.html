Actor Robert De Niro Welcomes His 7th Child at 79, Newborn Spotted for the First Time (Photos)


Robert De Niro’s newborn has been seen for the first time days after the actor revealed that he had welcomed his seventh child.

The iconic actor, 79, surprised the world by announcing the arrival of his new baby in a recent interview, confirming speculations about his girlfriend Tiffany Chen’s pregnancy after she was seen with a baby bump in March.

Proud mom Tiffany was photographed on Wednesday, May 10th, in NYC, cradling the newborn during a walk.

Following the announcement, De Niro told ET Canada, “I just had a baby,” surprising many with the news.

The Godfather actor corrected a reporter, stating that he is a father of seven, not six, emphasizing the recent addition to his family.

He also shared insights on parenting, admitting the challenges and the importance of doing the right thing for his children.

De Niro has children from previous relationships, including twins Aaron and Julian, 27, with former girlfriend, Toukie Smith, as well as two kids with his ex-wife Diahnne Abbott.
