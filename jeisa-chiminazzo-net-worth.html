What is Jeisa Chiminazzo's Net Worth?

Jeisa Chiminazzo is a Brazilian model who has a net worth of $8 million. Chiminazzo was born in Mucum, Rio Grande du Sol, Brazil in April 1985. At 13 she was discovered by an actor who recommender Jeisa to a modeling agency. She appeared on the cover of local magazines and then signed with IMG Models in New York. She appeared in Italian editions of Elle and Marie Claire magazines. She appeared in campaigns for Jill Stuart and Emanuel Ungaro.

She appeared on the cover of Paris Vogue in July 2005. To date she has appeared on the cover of nine international editions of Vogue which include Brazilian, British, French, Spanish, Japanese, and Russian. Chiminazzo has modeled to Calvin Klein, CK Jeans, Dolce & Gabbana, Hugo Boss, Armani, Karl Lagerfield, Yves Saint Laurent, Marc Jacobs, Dior, Chanel, Tiffany, DKNY, Vera Wang, Gap, Tommy Hilfiger, Levi, Tommy Jeans, Abercrombie, Nautica, Emporio Armani, Lacoste, and H&M. In 2006 she was featured in the Victoria's Secret Fashion Show. She also appeared in the 2008 Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue. Jeisa married Oliver Ripley in 2013 in a three day wedding that featured parties and fireworks.
