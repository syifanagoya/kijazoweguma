Nicole Catanese - InStyle

Location: Seaside Park, New Jersey

Expertise: Beauty, Fitness, Wellness

Experience

Nicole Catanese is a freelance writer and editor specializing in beauty, fitness and wellness. She has spent her career contributing to various editorial outlets including Harper's Bazaar, Self, Elle, Marie Claire, Glamour, Byrdie, and Cosmopolitan, as well as consulting with various brands to curate content. She currently lives in Seaside Park, New Jersey with her husband and two daughters.

About InStyle

InStyle is an award-winning destination for fashion, beauty, and pop-culture, with an all-are-welcome ethos that swings open the proverbial velvet ropes.
