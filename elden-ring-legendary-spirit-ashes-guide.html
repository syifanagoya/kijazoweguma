How to Find All of Elden Rings Legendary Spirit Ashes

Spread the love

In Elden Ring, there are a total of 9 Legendary Spirit Ashes to be found. They are all quite well hidden, but with a little bit of exploration, you’ll be able to track them all down. The following guide will show you where to find every single one.

The first Legendary Spirit Ashes can be found near the beginning of the game, in the area just after the tutorial section. Head east from the starting point and cross the river, then follow the path up into the mountains. Near the top of the mountain is a small cave – enter it and walk through to the other side. There, you’ll find a skeleton next to a pile of ashes – this is where you’ll find your first Legendary Spirit Ashes.

The next two Legendary Spirit Ashes can be found in the area just after the first major boss battle. Head east from the starting point and cross the river, then follow the path up into the mountains. Near the top of the mountain is a small cave – enter it and walk through to the other side. There, you’ll find a skeleton next to a pile of ashes – this is where you’ll find your second and third Legendary Spirit Ashes.

The next four Legendary Spirit Ashes can be found in the area just after the second major boss battle. Head east from the starting point and cross the river, then follow the path up into the mountains. Near the top of the mountain is a small cave – enter it and walk through to the other side. There, you’ll find a skeleton next to a pile of ashes – this is where you’ll find your fourth, fifth, sixth, and seventh Legendary Spirit Ashes.

The final two Legendary Spirit Ashes can be found in the area just after the third major boss battle. Head east from the starting point and cross the river, then follow the path up into the mountains. Near the top of the mountain is a small cave – enter it and walk through to the other side. There, you’ll find a skeleton next to a pile of ashes – this is where you’ll find your eighth and ninth Legendary Spirit Ashes.
