Homecoming actress Laura Harrier Boyfriend, Bio, Net Worth.

Short Wiki

The Spider-Man: Homecoming actress was born as Laura Ruth Harrier on March 28, 1990, in Chicago, Illinois, under the zodiac sign of Aries. She holds an American nationality and belongs to a mixed ethnic group.

Laura is the daughter of an Afro-American father Temujin Harrier and a Polish, English, mother, Linda Sagan Harrier. She has a younger brother named William Harrier. Concerning education, Laura graduated from Evanston Township High School.

Later, she studied art history at the New York University Gallatin School of Individualized Study. Similarly, Laura also took acting classes at William Esper Studio.
